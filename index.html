Kannan Narayanaswamy

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My research lies at the intersection of development economics and political economy. I use quasi-experimental methods to study education, health, labor, and public service delivery, with a particular focus on India.

Before beginning my PhD, I worked as a Research Associate at J-PAL South Asia on projects in education and early childhood development. I also worked as a consultant on water, sanitation and hygiene (WASH).
